## Full Description

Daily Healthy Bites brings you science-backed health and nutrition research — distilled into clear, actionable insights you can use today.

**What you'll find:**

🥗 **Nutrition Science** — Mediterranean diet research, gut health, anti-inflammatory foods
🔬 **Evidence-Based** — Every article cites peer-reviewed studies with links
💪 **Fitness & Longevity** — Exercise science, muscle preservation, healthy aging
🧠 **Mental Wellness** — Sleep optimization, stress management, brain health
🌿 **Natural Compounds** — Quercetin, spermidine, polyphenols — what the science actually shows

**Why Daily Healthy Bites?**

• Written by James Harper, PhD — a research scientist who reads the journals so you don't have to
• Every claim backed by PubMed-indexed studies
• No supplement pitches. No wellness fads. Just evidence.
• Updated regularly with the latest research
• Works offline — read saved articles anywhere
• Clean, distraction-free reading experience

**Topics include:**
GLP-1 drugs and muscle loss, Mediterranean diet longevity, microbiome testing (ZOE), spermidine and aging, quercetin and inflammation, and much more.
